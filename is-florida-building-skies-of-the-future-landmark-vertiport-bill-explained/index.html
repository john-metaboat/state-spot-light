Is Florida Building Skies of the Future? Landmark Vertiport Bill Explained!

Florida is looking towards the future of transportation as it introduces a groundbreaking bill focused on the development of vertiports. The bill, known as Section 332.15 of the Florida Statutes, aims to promote the establishment of a network of vertiports throughout the state, providing residents with access to advanced air mobility operations. This legislation defines and regulates the terms related to this new form of transportation, including "advanced air mobility" and "vertiport." Additionally, it outlines various measures to ensure public access and prevent monopolization or discrimination. With this bill, Florida is positioning itself as a pioneer in embracing innovative air transportation technologies.
